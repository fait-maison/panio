Panio
Why Panio?

I play piano for theatrical improv groups. Every night, a new scene unfolds and I need to score it live — like a film composer with no script. A love scene? Tender arpeggios. A chase? Driving octaves. The audience feels the shift before they understand it.

But that reflex — translating an emotion into the right mode, the right texture, instantly — doesn't come naturally. It takes practice. Panio is the tool I built to train it.
