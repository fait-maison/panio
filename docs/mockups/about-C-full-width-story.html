Panio

Score the moment.

Every improv scene deserves a live soundtrack.

I play piano for theatrical improv groups. Every night, a new scene unfolds and I need to score it live — like a film composer with no script.

A love scene? Tender arpeggios.
A chase? Driving octaves.
The audience feels the shift before they understand it.

But that reflex — translating an emotion into the right mode, the right texture, instantly — doesn't come naturally. It takes practice.

Panio is the tool I built to train it. It throws random musical ambiances at you — a mode, a key, a texture — and you play. No evaluation. No scoring. Just you and the moment.
